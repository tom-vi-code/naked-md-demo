"24/7 Digital Growth Engine" — an AI inbound & outbound sales agent covering the entire member lifecycle. Key pillars:

1. SELL & CONVERT

Sell memberships using current active offers
Issue free trials and guest passes instantly
Book facility tours and kickoff appointments
Distribute secure payment and signup links
2. SCHEDULE & RECOVER

Schedule and reschedule operational appointments
Detect no-shows automatically
Re-engage missed appointments immediately
3. NOTIFY & FOLLOW-UP

Send guest pass expiration reminders
Automate appointment confirmations
Execute win-back campaigns for lapsed members and leads
4. SUPPORT & INFORM

Provide instant club details, brand info, and pricing
Facilitate cross-channel link sharing
5. OMNICHANNEL

Web Chat + SMS + Voice (Call) — seamless cross-channel handoff
Human-in-the-loop escalation for high-intent/complex cases
6. WHO IT SERVES

New leads & guests (guest passes, first-time memberships)
Active & lapsed members (retention, rejoin)
Call-ins & web users (inbound capture)
7. STRATEGIC IMPACT (5 Pillars)

Zero Leakage — every lead addressed instantly
Revenue Protection — 24/7 opportunity capture
Staff Productivity — shift labor from repetitive to high-value
Scalable Growth — handle volume peaks without scaling headcount
Brand Consistency — uniform messaging across all locations
